# Racing All Along 🏎️
This repository is reflection of my passion for Motorsports `WEC`, `F1` and `MotoGP`. In this repository I have worked with open-source data available for each of the sports to debreif race-weekends, analyse the data to provide insight into the performance of the teams and drivers.

### Structure of the Repo 🏗️
- `F1`: Currently contains an in-depth race debrief for the Saudi Arabian GP 🇸🇦, 2025 and in-progress work debriefing the São Paulo GP 🇧🇷, 2025.
- `WEC`: Currently contains a introductory notebook for 8 Hours of Bahrain, 2025.

### Debriefs
The goal of the a race-weekend debrief in my work is analyse data collected independant of the teams over the weekend for estimating and visualising key performance variables such as:
- Car Setup and Circuit Characteristics corelation.
- Car Dyanmics and the effects on Race / Quali pace.
- Post-Race strategy and tyre degradation analysis.
- Point-Estimates to outline the best driving (bloated I know however, hence the challenge) over a race weekend.
